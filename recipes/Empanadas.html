Empanadas aka Pastelitos

Description

Fluffy pastry stuffed with delicious ground beef.

Ingredients

Beef filling:

  • 1/2 ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of oil
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste

Dough:

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 1/2 stick butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

    Beef filling:

  1. Heat a skillet with oil, and add in your beef
  2. Add your tomato paste
  3. Add minced garlic, salt and oregano
  4. Mix everything until cooked thoroughly
  5. Set aside for dough

    6. Dough:

  6. Mix your flour and softened butter with a fork
  7. Add 2 eggs and mix it in with your hands
  8. Add water, mixing until you can form a ball of dough - then let it sit covered for 15 minutes
  9. Roll the dough in a floured surface until it is about 1/8in thick
  10. Cut circles of about 6in of diameter
  11. Roll the excess dough again and keep cutting circles until all is used
  12. Fill the circles with your filling and then fold then in half like a moon - tip from my abuelita is to use a fork to create the creases on your half moon shape
  13. Now we will use a deep pan already hot with oil to fry them until they are golden brown.
