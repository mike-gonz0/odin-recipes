Papa-rellenas

Description

Breaded and pan friend, mashed potato balls stuffed with seasoned ground beef and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2lb russet potatoes - peeled and chopped into 8ths
  • water
  • 1/4 cup of oat milk
  • 2 tsp chopped garlic
  • 1 cup of chopped peppers
  • 1 diced yellow onion
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • pepper to taste
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • sunflower oil or vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. First we are going to make or filling, by adding oil to a deep and wide skillet, and browning our ground beef.
  2. Add - salt, garlic, chopped peppers to your ground beef, and saute until onions are clear.
  3. So now we make the potatoes, I use a regular pot and hot water - so in an pot I add enough water to cover my chopped potatoes, and then wait 15 minutes until the potatoes are easily pierced by a fork.
  4. Once, the potatoes are tender, drain the water and use a potato masher to mash the potatoes.
  5. Once, the mashed potatoes are chunky - add the milk, garlic, salt and pepper. Use the masher to mash until the potatoes are smooth.
  6. In your hand, use about 2 tbsp of mashed potatoes. Form them into your hand, in a bowl shape. Place a spoonful of your ground beef mix in the center of the bowl. Fold the mashed potatoes over, closing the picadillo inside.
  7. In a separate bowl, whisk up an egg.
  8. Place the breadcrumbs in a shallow bowl.
  9. Now the magic begins - roll each mashed potato ball in the whisked egg. Then roll it in the breadcrumbs.
  10. Finally, heat another pan with oil to about 375 degrees, and place each breaded mashed potato ball in the oil and allow to fry for about 2 minutes on each side until it turns golden brown. Enjoy
