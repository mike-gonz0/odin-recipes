Vegan Lasagna

Description

A lasagna that my whole family will eat without complaining.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried red lentils
  • 2x 25 ounce marinara sauce - not ragu.
  • 1 cup raq cashews
  • 16 ounce firm tofu, patted dry with paper towels
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice or 2 lemons
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2-3 cups baby spinach

Steps

  1. Lets start off by cooking the red lentils - add 1 cup dried lentils and 3 cups of water to a medium pot let it boil and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Once the lentils are done, drain them, add to a large bow, and add your marinara.
  3. Pre-heat the Over to 370 degrees.
  4. Now lets dive into making the cashew-tofu ricotta - add the cashews into a blender and process(pulse) until it is finely crumbled. Then add the chunks of tofu, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, salt, basil, oregano, and garlic powder to the blender. Pulse until it is all well combined and smooth.
  5. Let's assemble the lasagna - add about 1 cup of marinara with lentils to the bottom of a large 9x13 inch casserole dish or pan making sure to spread it evenly.
  6. first layer of lasagna noodle layered by half of your cashew tofu ricotta spread, and finally spinach.
  7. Then repeat the process above with your final layer being ricota and spinach mix.
