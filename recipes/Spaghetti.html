Spaghetti

Description

Dominican style spaghetti with salami typical of the island and olives, in a red paste.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Pasta(your choice)
  • 1 1/2 cups diced Dominican salami
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 minced green or red pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp of chicken bullion or adobo
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
    • <1i>1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 minced onion
  • olives to taste
  • water from pasta

Steps

  1. Dice the salami into roughly 1in pieces set aside
  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil with salt and add pasta. Cook pasta to desired doneness - preferably al dente.
  3. In a deep skillet over medium-high heat, add 1 tsp of oil and salami - cook evenly on all sides.
  4. Next add your onions, peppers, and garlic - saute until the veggies have softened, then add your adobo, oregano, and olives.
  5. Now add your tomato paste and sauce right after, be sure to work it into the veggies and salami as best you can - then add 1/2 cup of your pasta water.
  6. Now that the sauce is ready, add cooked spaghetti and gently fold into the sauce until all of the noodle is coated with the spaghetti sauce.
