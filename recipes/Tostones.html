Tostones

Description

Crispy, savory and delicious twice pan fried plantains, are the snack of choice in the Caribbean

Ingredients

  • 4 green plantains
  • 1 cup vegetable oil or canola oil as needed
  • 4 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt, plus more to top
  • 6 limes

Steps

  1. cut and peal your plantains - cut the ends, then slice through the middle, and pry the skin off
  2. cut the bare plantain into round medallions
  3. fill a deep skillet with about 1cm of oil
  4. while your pan warms, mix 1/2cup of water, garlic, kosher salt, and lime juice
  5. now add your plantains to the pan with oil - flipping them over after 5 minutes
  6. once both sides have been cooked for 5 minutes per medallion you will need to bring them out of the pan
  7. next use the bottom of a glass, and gently flatten each medallion
  8. once flattened, you will need to add each of the medallions back into the pan to be fried for an additional 1 minutes each
  9. now transfer them to a paper towel lined plate
  10. sprinkle a little salt on them, bring out the sauce you made with the garlic and lime juice and BAM!
